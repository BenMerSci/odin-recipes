Fresh corn salad

Fresh salad to cool down on a hot summer day

This fresh corn salad coupled with tomatoes and cucumber will surely amaze everyone!

Ingredients

  1. 2 ears corn, kernels cut from the cob
  2. 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  3. 1 cucumber, halved lengthwise and sliced
  4. 1 avocado, diced
  5. 1 small sweet onion, chopped
  6. Â¼ bunch fresh cilantro, chopped, or to taste
  7. 1 green onion, green parts only, thinly sliced
  8. 1 drizzle extra-virgin olive oil
  9. 1 lime
  10. salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix corn kernels, tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, onion, cilantro, and green onion together in a large bowl. Add a drizzle of olive oil. Squeeze lime juice into the bowl and season with salt and pepper.
